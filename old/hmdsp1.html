Happy Mother's Day!

Oh mom this is your day,
let your worries wash away.
Banish all your tears,
abolish all your fears.
Today is your day to to do what you wish,
today is your day to have fun.
Today is your day to relax,
not to worry about getting things done.
Let your spirit lift,
free from burdens and chores.
Be happy and and enjoy the day,
for this one is yours.
