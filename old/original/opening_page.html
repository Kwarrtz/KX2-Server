Prototype Webserver




About the Webserver:

The RasPi_1 Prototype Webserver was first brought online on January 24th, 2013 by Dathan and Brett Ault-McCoy. It is made entirely from Python and HTML and is running on a Raspberry Pi Machine. (Hence the name: RasPi_1.) Although it is still a work in progress, it has several pages linked to it and we are adding more. We are hoping to have it running consitantly except for occasional additions to the webserver by the end of March.
